Return Policy

Returns, Refunds & Cancellations

  • Products can be returned within 7 days of receiving only if the packs are sealed and not damaged in any way.

  • MOTO-GARAGE team must receive and approve your return request. Once your request is received and approved, we will arrange for a return pick up at an agreed time.

  • In the unlikely event that your products arrive damaged, the customers are required to E-Mail us a picture of the damaged product with the Packaging and Bill Details to customer.service@moto-garage.com, within 48 hours of receiving your order.

  • Upon authorization of returns, we will offer you an exchange or refund. You could choose to get a full refund in the form of store credits or get a refund on your original credit or debit card used for payment in which case return shipping charges and a stocking fee might be applicable. Items that may have been damaged, defective or sent by error will be fully refunded. Refund will be processed within 7 working days from the date we receive the products back.

  • We aim to process all returns within one (1) week. If you have any questions about your return, please create a ticket by clicking here.
  • All returns are subject to the discretion of GrandPitstop.com.

  • Orders which are pending for shipping can be cancelled within 2 working days from the date of placing the order. Once the order is cancelled, the refund will be processed within 7 working days.

  • .For Product Replacement cases, new product would be sent by GrandPitstop once it receives the original product from the customer. This process would take around 8-10 working days.

  • For Refunds, Once the product is returned and delivered to GrandPitstop , it takes 7-8 working days to initiate the Refund to customer's account .
